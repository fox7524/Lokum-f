NARESH KUMAR PUGLIA

(a) whether the Global lodine Deficiency Disorders Day is observed on the 2/st October; 

(b) if so, the details of programmes launched to control the disease; 

(c) the amount spent on National lodine Deficiency Disorders Control Programme (NIDDCP) during each of the last three years;
