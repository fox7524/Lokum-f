UNSTARRED QUESTION NO:738 
ANSWERED ON:21.07.2016 
Ocean Energy 

Gandhi Dr. Dharam Vira 


Will the Minister of NEW AND RENEWABLE ENERGY be pleased to state: 


a) whether the Government has taken any steps to make ocean energy, especially tidal energy, more feasible in terms of cost and 
technology; 

b) if so, the details thereof; and 

c) the steps taken/being taken by the Government to ensure that marine biodiversity is not hampered owing to ocean energy 
generation? 


Answer 


MINISTER OF STATE FOR POWER, COAL , NEW & RENEWABLE ENERGY & MINES (SHRI PYUSH GOYAL) 

(a)&(b): The National Clean Energy Fund (NCEF) is specially created to cater innovative projects under clean energy. Secondly, the 
Ministry of New & Renewable Energy (MNRE) supports the promotion of Research, Design & Development for projects including 
ocean(tidal energy also through financial incentives. 

(c): The MNRE follows the steps as per guidelines of Ministry of Environment and Forest & Climate Change on the subject to ensure 
that marine biodiversity is not hampered owing to ocean energy generation.
