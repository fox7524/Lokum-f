290 (522) : Sri Darmasingh Hon'ble Chief Minister? 
a) Whether it has come to the notice of the Government that names 
were left out from the electoral rolls during the last general elections? 
b) What actions have been taken up in this regard? 
c) Is ita fact that every voter has not been issued the voters’ 1D 
card properly? 


726 LEGISLATIVE ASSEMBLY 


d) When would the programme of issue of voter's ID cards be 
completed? 

Sri Ashok R. (Uttarahalli) 

a) Yes. This phenomenon has been noticed in some urban areas 
of a few cities like Bangalore, etc. 

b) Petitions have been received from general public regarding 
missing of names from the voters' lists. The concerned District Election 
Officers have been instructed to find out the reasons for delection of 
names from the voters' lists and to take action to include the missing 
names in the concerned voters' lists, as per rules. Further, revision of 
electroral rolls with reference to 1-1-2005 as the qualifying date is being 
taken up shortly as per the direction of the Election Commission of 
India and the names‘ of all the left out voters will be included in the 
voters’ lists at that time. 

c) Every voter has to come to the concerned polling Station (which 
is also called Designated Photography Location) to get himself 
potographed and to get the cards. So far 81% of the voters' have been 
issued EPICs. 

d) The campaign of issue of voters’ identity cards is a continuous 
process since fresh enrollment to the voters list will be made every 
year.
